Bonuses

The true heart-pounding excitement in Le Digger lies within its lucrative bonus features. The base game is engaging, but the special features are where the massive 15,000x wins are hiding.

Free Spins Feature
Landing 3 or more Scatter symbols anywhere on the reels will trigger the highly anticipated Free Spins round. During this mode, the game's mechanics often shift in the player's favor. You might encounter increasing multipliers, sticky wilds that remain in place for the duration of the feature, or expanding symbols.

Wilds & Multipliers
Even outside the main bonus game, Wild symbols play a crucial role. They not only substitute for regular symbols but may also carry multiplier values that can significantly boost standard line wins.
