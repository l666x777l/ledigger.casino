How to Play

Getting started with Le Digger is incredibly straightforward, thanks to Hacksaw Gaming's intuitive user interface.

Step 1: Set Your Bet
Before spinning the reels, use the + and - buttons at the bottom of the screen to adjust your bet size. Always choose a stake that fits your bankroll.

Step 2: Spin the Reels
Hit the Spin button to set the game in motion. You can also use the Autoplay feature to play a set number of spins automatically.

Step 3: Form Winning Combinations
Wins are awarded by landing matching symbols across the game's paylines or cluster grid. Keep an eye out for high-paying premium symbols and Wilds, which substitute for other symbols to help complete winning combinations.
